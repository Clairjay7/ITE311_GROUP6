About St. Peter Hospital Inc.

St. Peter Hospital Inc. operates a main hospital and several affiliate clinics across General Santos City, serving hundreds of patients daily. With plans to establish an additional hospital outside the city, we are committed to providing integrated healthcare solutions through our advanced Hospital Management System (HMS).
